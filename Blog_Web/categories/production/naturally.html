G19 - Naturally dried banana strings
  • Admin
  • March 03, 2024

Natural fiber made from dried banana strings is a natural product derived from banana trees, mainly from dried banana strings, a very popular plant in Vietnam. Natural fibers from dried banana vines are created without chemical processes or harsh treatment, retaining the natural properties of the banana plant.
The natural color and beauty are often brown or dark brown, creating a natural and impressive look. This is often appreciated in craft design and fashion production. Its versatility allows it to be used in a variety of applications, including the production of handmade products, mattresses, chairs and even home decoration.
Dried banana rope has natural durability and elasticity, making the product easily resistant to sun, rain and wear over time. Natural fibers from dried banana vines are recyclable and naturally biodegradable, making them an environmentally friendly choice. Natural fibers from dried banana vines are often used in interior design and fashion to create a natural style, close to nature. Natural fibers from dried banana strings not only bring naturalness and environmental friendliness, but also create impression and creativity in many different applications.
