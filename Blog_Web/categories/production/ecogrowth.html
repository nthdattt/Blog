PRODUCTION

EcoGrowth - Retail businesses provide packaging for small supermarket merchants (domestic and international).
  • Admin
  • March 03, 2024

Date of establishment: May 9, 2023
Fanpage: EcoGrowth Package
Products: EcoGrowth Package (EGP), Paper packaging combined with seedlings.
Vision:
- Create packaging products made from recycled paper, environmentally friendly, capable of growing into trees after being planted in the ground. This not only helps grow more trees but is also environmentally friendly.
Mission:
- Introducing creative packaging products: making paper packaging more unique and easier to attract more buyers and users by adding the function of being able to grow trees, fruit trees, and flowers .
- Guide people towards a green lifestyle: after using paper packaging, instead of throwing it away, you can keep it and plant it in the ground, which is both environmentally friendly, reduces waste, and plants more trees.
- Propaganda to raise awareness of environmental protection.
- Providing value for tourism, export and daily consumption.
- Future directions will include more product types beyond packaging.
